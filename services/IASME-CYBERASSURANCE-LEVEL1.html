IASME Cyber Assurance Level 1 Certification

IASME Cyber Assurance Level 1 is designed for small businesses and organisations seeking to demonstrate their commitment to essential cybersecurity measures. This entry-level certification confirms your organisation follows good security practices and protects sensitive information.

What is IASME Cyber Assurance Level 1?

Level 1 Cyber Assurance focuses on practical cybersecurity controls that help businesses protect their digital assets. It is ideal for organisations seeking reassurance of security without the need for intensive technical assessments. Certification highlights your commitment to safeguarding data and reducing cyber risks.

Key Features of Level 1

  • Basic Security Controls: Covers firewalls, password management, and software updates.
  • Staff Awareness: Ensures employees understand cybersecurity risks and best practices.
  • Access Management: Protects sensitive information by managing user permissions effectively.
  • Data Handling: Introduces practical measures to safeguard personal and business data.
  • Simple Risk Assessment: Identifies common cyber risks and outlines mitigation strategies.
