Cyber Essentials Plus Certification

Take your cybersecurity a step further with Cyber Essentials Plus, the advanced UK government-backed certification. It shows that your business not only implements essential security measures but also validates them through independent testing.

What is Cyber Essentials Plus?

Cyber Essentials Plus builds upon the standard Cyber Essentials framework, providing additional assurance through hands-on technical verification. Systems are independently tested by qualified assessors to confirm that your security measures are correctly implemented and effective.

Enhanced Security Controls

  • Technical Verification: Independent testing of firewalls, malware protection, and user access controls.
  • Advanced Malware Checks: Real-world malware testing ensures systems are properly protected.
  • Configuration Validation: Assessors confirm devices and software are securely configured.
  • Patch Verification: Checks that updates and security patches are applied correctly.
  • Continuous Improvement: Identify vulnerabilities and receive guidance for enhancing cybersecurity.

Why Get Cyber Essentials Plus Certified?

  • Provides independent assurance of your cybersecurity measures.
  • Reduces the risk of data breaches more effectively than standard Cyber Essentials.
  • Enhances credibility with customers, suppliers, and stakeholders.
  • Meets more stringent government and contractual security requirements.
  • Demonstrates your proactive approach to protecting sensitive information.
