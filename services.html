Services

We offer a number of IT services and solutions, just as it states in the name! Have a look below on exactly what we can provide, you won’t be disappointed in our service…

Cyber Essentials

The foundation of every cyber-secure business. Cyber Essentials focuses on key technical controls to protect against the most common cyber threats. It's government-backed, affordable, and ideal for demonstrating basic cyber hygiene.

  • Self-assessment with IASME verified review
  • Reduce cyber risks by up to 80%
  • Mandatory for many UK government contracts
  • Includes certification badge valid for 12 months

Cyber Essentials Plus

For businesses seeking deeper assurance, Cyber Essentials Plus includes hands-on technical verification by certified assessors. It proves your defences are robust and effective against real-world attacks.

  • Independent vulnerability testing
  • Enhanced credibility with partners and clients
  • Comprehensive audit and report
  • Qualifies for advanced supply chain contracts
Book an Assessment

IASME Cyber Assurance Level 1

CyberOps helps you achieve IASME Cyber Assurance Level 1, covering people, processes, and technology. This certification demonstrates strong governance and risk management — perfect for SMEs wanting to go beyond the basics.

  • Includes data protection, risk, and access controls
  • Assessor-verified self-assessment
  • Aligns with GDPR and ISO principles
  • Scalable to IASME Level 2 (audited)
